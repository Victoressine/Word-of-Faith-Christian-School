Admissions

Welcome to Word Of Faith Christian School – join our nurturing community from Preschool to Senior High School

Admissions Overview

At Word Of Faith Christian School, we welcome students from Preschool (Crèche & Nursery) through to Primary, Junior High School (JHS), and Senior High School (SHS). Our admissions process is straightforward, transparent, and designed to help parents and guardians make the right choice for their child’s education. Enrolment is open throughout the academic year depending on available spaces.

Application Process

  1. Obtain and complete the Application Form from the school office or download it below.
  2. Submit the completed form with all required documents and photographs.
  3. Pay the non-refundable admission and assessment fee.
  4. Applicants to Primary, JHS and SHS will sit for an entrance assessment in English, Mathematics, and Science.
  5. Successful candidates will receive an Admission Letter confirming placement, fees, and start date.
  6. Registration is completed after payment of entry fees and purchase of uniforms and books.
Download Application Form

Admission Requirements

All applicants to Word Of Faith Christian School must provide the following:

  • Completed admission form
  • Two (2) passport-sized photographs of the child
  • Photocopy of birth certificate or age declaration
  • Immunization records (for preschool applicants)
  • Most recent academic report card (for Primary, JHS & SHS applicants)
  • Transfer certificate from previous school (where applicable)
