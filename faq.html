Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions, academics, and more.

Applicants must submit a completed admission form, a birth certificate, previous school reports/results, and two passport-sized photographs. All students are also required to take an entrance exam and/or attend an interview.
Yes, Word of Faith Christian School provides safe and well-supervised boarding facilities with a nurturing environment that supports both academic and spiritual growth.
We run the Oxford International Curriculum alongside the Ghana Education Service (GES) standards, enriched with Christian values. The program integrates academics, ICT, leadership, moral education, and life skills.
Students participate in sports (football, volleyball, athletics, table tennis), cultural activities (drama, music, dance), academic clubs (debate, science, quiz), and faith-based groups such as Scripture Union and Bible study.
Admission forms can be downloaded from our website or collected at the school office. Completed forms should be submitted with the required documents. Successful applicants will be contacted after the entrance exam and interview.
