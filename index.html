Headmaster's Address

Welcome to Word of Faith Christian School. Here, we believe every child is a gift from God, created with unique potential to impact the world. Since our establishment in 1989, our Christ-centered community has been committed to nurturing students in faith, knowledge, and character.

We combine strong academic foundations with spiritual growth, guided by biblical principles and discipline in love. From Preschool to Senior High School, our students benefit from the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum as well as our newly accredited Oxford International Curriculum, preparing them to excel both locally and globally.

At Word of Faith Christian School, our mission is to develop the whole child—mind, body, and spirit—equipping them to shine as lights in their generation. We are honoured to partner with parents in raising students who will lead with wisdom, faith, and integrity.

About Us

Our Story & Heritage

Welcome to Word of Faith Christian School. Since our founding in 1989, we have been committed to raising generations of students who excel academically, grow spiritually, and uphold moral integrity. Rooted in Christian values, our school provides a nurturing environment where children are encouraged to dream, believe, achieve, and inspire.

Our Mission & Vision

  • Mission: “To provide a Christ-centered, holistic education that fosters academic excellence, moral integrity, and global citizenship.”
  • Vision: “To inspire a community of lifelong learners who embody Christian values, achieve academic excellence, uphold moral integrity, and contribute positively as global citizens.”
