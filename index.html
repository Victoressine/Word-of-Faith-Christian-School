Headmaster's Address/Welcome Message

Welcome to Word of Faith Christian School. At Word of Faith Christian School, we nurture every child to shine brightly in faith, knowledge and character. Established in 1989, our Christ-centered institution delivers academic excellence and spiritual growth in a loving, disciplined environment. From Nursery to Senior High School, we integrate biblical principles with a robust curriculum, including our newly accredited Oxford International Curriculum, alongside the Ghana Education Service (GES) standards, to develop the whole child—mind, body, and spirit.
