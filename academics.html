Your child’s journey begins here

Selecting the perfect school for your child is a pivotal choice for every parent. At Word Of Faith Christian School, we are committed to guiding you through this journey with programs designed to nurture your child’s full potential, values, and faith.

We aim to be one of the best Christian schools in Ghana, dedicated to creating a nurturing and stimulating environment where young minds can thrive academically, socially, and spiritually. Our programs run year-round, providing a supportive space for learners, closing only during major holiday seasons and public holidays to allow families time together.

Preschool / Nursery

At Word Of Faith Christian School, our Preschool and Nursery programs are designed to spark curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning in our youngest learners. We focus on building strong foundations academically, socially, and spiritually.

Ages: 3 to 5 years. Programs focus on literacy, numeracy, communication skills, and emotional development.

Activities include play-based learning, creative arts, music, storytelling, basic science experiments, and early motor skills development.

Requirements & Form

Primary School

At Word Of Faith Christian School, our Primary School program builds on the foundations laid in Nursery, nurturing academic excellence, creativity, and strong moral values.

Ages: 6 to 11 years. Curriculum emphasizes literacy, numeracy, critical thinking, and social-emotional development.

Programs include English and Mathematics mastery, Science and Technology exploration, Creative Arts, Physical Education, and Biblical Studies. Each child receives personalized guidance from our qualified teachers to ensure holistic growth.

Requirements & Form

Junior High School

The Junior High School at Word Of Faith Christian School serves students aged 12 to 15 years, providing a bridge between primary education and senior high school.

Curriculum includes advanced literacy and numeracy, Science, ICT, Creative Arts, Physical Education, and Biblical Studies. Students engage in critical thinking projects, teamwork, and leadership development.

Requirements & Form

Senior High School

Senior High School at Word Of Faith Christian School serves students aged 16 to 18 years. Focus is on preparation for tertiary education, career guidance, and advanced learning.

Programs include advanced Mathematics, Sciences, ICT, Creative Arts, Physical Education, and Biblical Studies. Students also engage in leadership and personal development programs.
