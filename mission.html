Our History, Mission & Vision

Discover the values and principles that guide Word of Faith Christian School.

Our Story & Heritage

Word of Faith Christian School was established to provide quality Christ-centered education rooted in academic excellence, discipline, and moral integrity. Our heritage is built on nurturing students to grow in knowledge, faith, and leadership, preparing them to excel in every sphere of life.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic Christian education that develops the mind, body, and spirit. We are committed to empowering students with academic knowledge, practical skills, and biblical values to influence society positively.

Our Vision

To be a distinguished Christian school known for academic excellence, strong moral values, and spiritual growth, raising future leaders who will impact the world with wisdom, creativity, and godly character.
